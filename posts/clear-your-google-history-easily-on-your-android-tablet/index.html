3. Delete Google Search History#

Google History allows users to keep track of and store information from their activity. It can include search terms, webpages they’ve viewed, videos watched, and locations they’ve visited. It is important to know how to delete Google History so that all of the activities on the device stay secure. Unfortunately, this feature doesn’t seem to be available on Android tablets yet. Here is a guide on how to delete Google History on your Android tablet.

The first step is to open the Google app on your Android tablet. On the main page, tap on the “Settings” icon in the upper right corner. Here, you can access various settings for your account. Scroll down until you see “Google History” and tap on it.

On the next page, you will see a list of all the data that has been stored in your Google History. Here, you can select the data that you want to delete or the entire list. To delete the data, simply tap the “Delete” or “Delete All” button at the bottom of the page.

Once you have deleted the data, you can go back to the main page and turn on the “Do Not Track” feature. This will prevent Google from tracking your activities on your Android tablet. It is important to note that this feature does not delete the data that has already been stored in your Google History.

Finally, you can go back to the settings page and turn off the “Google History” feature. This will ensure that no new data is added to your Google History. With these simple steps, you can make sure that your activities on your Android tablet remain private and secure.

Clear Your Google History Easily On Your Android Tablet. Clearing your Google history on your android tablet is easy. Here are the steps: First, open the Google app on your tablet. Finally, confirm that you want to turn this feature off and all your data will be removed from Google history.
