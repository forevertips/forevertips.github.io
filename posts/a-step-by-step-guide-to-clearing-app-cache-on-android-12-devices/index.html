1. Understanding App Cache#

Clearing app cache on Android 12 devices is essential for the smooth functioning of your device. It can help you free up valuable storage space, and allow you to access more content. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you clear your app cache.

First, tap on the Settings icon on your device. Then, locate Apps or Applications, and tap it. From here, you can view all the installed apps on your device. Select the one you want to clear cached data, and tap on Storage. Here you will find the option to clear the app cache.

Tap on the Clear Cache button to delete the stored data, and then check the data usage of the app. If it hasn’t changed significantly, then the app cache was successfully cleared. If it hasn’t, then you may need to delete the app and reinstall it.

It is important to regularly check your device for cached data, and clear it. This will ensure that your phone will run faster, and you will have access to more apps and contents. Lastly, make sure to set a reminder to regularly clear your app cache!
