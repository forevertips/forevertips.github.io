  2. Tap “Chat history” and then select “Export chat”.
  3. Choose “Export to Google Drive” and authenticate with your Google account.
  4. On your iPhone, open WhatsApp and tap “Settings”.
  5. Select “Chats” and then go to “Chat history”.
  6. Tap “Import chat” and select “Import from Google Drive”.
  7. Select the “WhatsApp” folder you just created on your Android device and authenticate with your Google account.
