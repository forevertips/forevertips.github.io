2. Steps to Record a Phone Call on Samsung S21#

Recording phone calls on Android Samsung S21 is a great way to keep a record of important conversations, for use in business or personal settings. Recording a call is easier than ever on the new Samsung S21, thanks to the built-in “Call Recording” feature. Here’s a quick guide on how to record a phone call on your Samsung S21.

To start, open the Phone app on your Samsung S21 and dial the person or number you want to call. When the call is connected, tap the More button (three vertical dots) at the top of the screen and select Call Recording. You should see a pop-up window informing you that your phone call will be recorded. Tap Ok to confirm.

During the call, you can press the Stop Recording button to end the recording. The Call Recording feature will save the recording to your device in MP3 format by default. To access the recording, open the Files app and go to Internal Storage > Audio > Call. You should be able to find the recording in that folder.

You can also share the recording with another party by long-pressing the recording and selecting Share. You can choose to share the recording via SMS messages, email, or any other sharing options available. There you have it: you now know how to record a phone call with your Samsung S21.
