1. Preparing Your Dell PC#

Preparing your Dell PC to take screenshots is easy and straightforward. The first step is to run the Snipping Tool application from the Start Menu. This will open the Snipping Tool window which shows the options “New”, “Cancel”, and “Options”. Click on “New” to continue. You will then be able to select the area of the screen you would like to capture as the screenshot. Once you have selected the area, click the “Save” button at the top to save the screenshot. You can then open the screenshot in your favorite image editor and apply any desired changes.

The second step is to use the Print Screen key. This is the quickest way to take a screenshot of your entire screen. To do this, press the Print Screen button on your keyboard and the screenshot will be automatically saved in your Pictures folder. You can open this image in your image editor and make any desired adjustments.

The third option is to use the Windows + Print Screen key combination. This will capture a screenshot of your desktop and also save it into your Pictures folder. This is the same process as above but faster and more efficient. You can open this image in your image editor and make any desired adjustments.

Finally, the fourth option is to use the Alt + Print Screen key combination. This will take a screenshot of the active window on your desktop and it will be saved in your Pictures folder. Again, you can open this image in your image editor and make any desired adjustments. Following these four simple steps, you can easily take screenshots on your Dell PC.

For those who would rather save the image to a file, just press the “Fn” key and the “Alt” key while pressing the “PrtSc” key. This will cause the image to be saved as a file in your computer’s Pictures folder.

Lastly, for those who wish to take a screenshot of a single window, simply press the “Fn” and the “Alt” keys together and press the “PrtSc” key while simultaneously pressing the “Windows” key. This will save an image of the active window only.
