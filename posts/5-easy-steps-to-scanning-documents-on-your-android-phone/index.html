Scanner apps are powerful tools which allow users to scan, store, and share documents from an Android phone. CamScanner is the most downloaded and used scanner app on the Google Play Store. It is an all-in-one app that can scan, store, organize, share, and sync your documents. With its OCR (Optical Character Recognition) feature, it can also turn printed documents into editable digital formats, allowing you to easily edit or access them on the go.

Adobe Scan is a powerful scanner app made by Adobe. It lets you scan documents to high quality PDFs or JPEG files with its advanced image processing technology. It is also integrated with Adobe Document Cloud, which makes it easy to store, search, and access scanned documents.

Office Lens is an app from Microsoft designed for scanning documents on the go. It has an Optical Character Recognition feature that turns paper documents into digital formats, as well as a built-in document scanner that turns photos into PDFs.

Tiny Scanner is a lightweight scanner app that allows you to convert photos to PDF files quickly and easily. Its robust scanner features can produce high-quality documents in a snap.

Genius Scan is the perfect scanner app for busy professionals. It allows you to quickly scan documents and turn them into JPEG or PDF files. It also has powerful document editing features that let you make quick adjustments to scanned documents before sharing them.

2. How To Prepare Your Document#

Scanners are essential to filing, archiving and sharing documents. With today’s technology, you don’t need to rush to the store and buy a physical scanner, as you can easily use your android phone. Here is a guide to help you scan your documents quickly and easily.

First, you need to install the right app. A good option is the Google Drive App. Once you have downloaded and installed it, you need to open the camera from within the app. From the camera settings, select document mode and make sure your document is properly aligned and framed.

Second, you need to adjust the scan settings for the best results. Through the app, you can select color modes, change photo size and adjust the sharpness of the document.

Third, simply tap the screen to capture the image. You can take more than one shot of the same document, and choose the best later.

Fourth, after you have taken the shot, use the editing tools to edit your document. Tools such as cropping, color adjustment and noise removal will help you get the most ideal document.

Finally, you can save your document in Google Drive, or send it to someone via email. With these 5 easy steps, you can quickly and easily scan your documents on your Android phone.

Preparing documents to scan on your Android phone is easy and efficient. To begin, make sure the document you want to scan is placed on a flat, well-lit surface. Next check that all the content of the document is visible and legible. Then, use the camera of your Android phone to take a picture of the document. You may need to make adjustments according to the quality of the photo taken. Make sure to stay as still as possible during this process.
