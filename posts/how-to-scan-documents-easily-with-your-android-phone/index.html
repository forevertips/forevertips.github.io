1. Preparation for Scanning Documents With Android Phone#

Scanning documents is an incredibly useful task that can be done quickly and easily with an Android smartphone. It doesn’t require any expensive equipment or software, but it does require some preparation. To ensure that your documents are scanned accurately, here are the steps to take before you start scanning documents with your Android phone.

First, make sure that your camera is clean and free from smudges or dirt. To do this, simply wipe the lens with a clean microfiber cloth. A dirty lens can lead to distorted or blurry images when scanning documents.

Second, check the lighting in the room. A bright, even light source will make sure that the image is clear and accurate. If the lighting is too dim, the image can be distorted or too dark.

Third, turn on the camera’s stabilizer and autofocus features. This will ensure that the image will remain stable and that the focus will remain on the document or item being scanned.

Finally, check the background of the image to make sure it is consistent and uncluttered. This will help ensure that the image is as clear and accurate as possible. Following these simple steps will help you get the best results when using your Android phone for document scanning.
