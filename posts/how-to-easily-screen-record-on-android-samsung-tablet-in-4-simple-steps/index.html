2. Steps to Record Screen on Android Samsung Tablet#

Recording your screen can be really helpful when you want to show other people how to complete a process or just to capture something you’ve been working on. We’ll show you how to easily screen record on Android Samsung Tablet in 4 simple steps.

First, go to the Settings on your Android Samsung Tablet. Then, scroll down and openings the Advanced Features. In Advanced Features, enable Smart capture. This will enable you to record your screen.

Then, swipe down from the top of the screen to open the Quick Settings menu. Tap on the Screen Recorder icon and a pop up window will appear on the screen. In the pop up window select the quality of the video and press Start.

Your recording will start and you can stop the recording by swiping from top to bottom again. Select Stop from the Screen Recorder icon menu again.

That’s it! You’ve just successfully recorded your screen on your Android Samsung Tablet. Now you can share it to other people or just save it for yourself.
