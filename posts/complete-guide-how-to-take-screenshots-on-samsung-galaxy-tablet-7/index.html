To begin, you’ll need to locate the volume down and power buttons. The volume down button is found on the left side of the tablet and the power button is found on the top edge. Simultaneously, press and hold the volume down and power buttons for a few seconds, and then release the buttons. The screenshot image will be saved in the My Files folder.

The Galaxy tablet 7 also provides an alternative method to take screenshots. Navigate to the desired page you wish to capture. Swipe the screen from one side to the other using your four fingertips. You can also utilize the Samsung screenshots app. This is a pre-installed application that is available for free on the Google Play Store. Install and open the app, then use your four fingers to swipe across the screen. The screenshot will be saved in the app.

These are the three different methods for taking screenshots on a Samsung Galaxy Tablet 7. Utilize any of the three options to capture and save screenshots with ease.
