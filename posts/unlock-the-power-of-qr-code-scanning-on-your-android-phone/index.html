1. How to enable QR code scanning on android phone#

QR (Quick Response) codes are popping up all around us, from magazines to billboards, and they’re offering a convenient way to access information. To unlock their power on your Android phone, you need to enable QR code scanning. Here’s how you can do it:

First, open the camera app on your Android phone and tap on the settings icon. On the next screen, enable the toggle next to Scan QR codes. This will allow the camera to scan QR codes when you point the camera at one.

Next, open your phone’s settings and find the Google Lens app. If you don’t have the app, you can download it from the Play Store. Once you open the app, select the Scan QR code option. You can now use the app to scan the QR code.

Lastly, you can also use a third-party QR code scanner app to scan the QR code. Simply open the app and point your camera at the QR code and it will scan it for you.

With QR code scanning enabled on your Android phone, you can now access any information stored in the QR code quickly and easily.
