1. Introduction on How to Transfer WhatsApp Chats from Android#

Transferring WhatsApp chats from an old Android to a new one is relatively easy now. You can either use Google Drive or a computer to do the job. Here we will be discussing the method of transfer using Google Drive. Firstly, you will need to have the Google Drive app installed on both the old and the new Android devices. After that, open the WhatsApp app on the old device and go to the Settings. You will find an option of Chats and tap the Chat Backup option. Then tap the ‘Back Up’ button and your chats will automatically be backed up to the linked Google Drive account. Once it is completed, make sure to disconnect the Google Drive account from the old phone. Now move onto the new Android device. Install WhatsApp on the device and open the app. This time you may be asked to either enter your old phone number or your Google accounts login credentials. After that, tap ‘Restore’ and all the previous conversations will be restored on your new device.

Next, move the backup file to the new device. To do this, you can connect both devices to a computer and manually transfer it over. Or, you can copy the backup file over a Bluetooth connection.

Finally, open WhatsApp on your new device and go to Settings > Chats > Chat Backup and select Restore. This will restore the chats from your old device to your new one. And that’s it - you have now transferred WhatsApp chats successfully!
