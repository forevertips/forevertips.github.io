Taking screenshots on iPad Air 2 can be easily done with just a few steps. Here’s a quick guide on the easiest way to take screenshots on your iPad Air 2.

First, press the Home and Power buttons at the same time. The iPad Air 2 will take a screenshot and save it in the Photos app. You can also find it in the Screenshots folder in your Albums.

Second, open the Photos app and select the screenshot. Tap the Share button and select the file format you prefer. You can choose JPG, PNG, or PDF.

Third, you’ll need to decide which folder to save the file in. You can save it in the iCloud Photos, albums, or on your device. Once you have chosen the folder, the screenshot will be automatically sent there.

Finally, you can open the screenshot in any third-party app and make further edits if you need to. The screenshot is also accessible from any other device you’re logged in to that is linked to the same Apple ID.

By following this simple guide, you can easily take and store screenshots on your iPad Air 2 in the file format you need.
