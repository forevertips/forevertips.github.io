Feeling overwhelmed to transfer all your contacts from your Apple iPhone 6s to your new Android device? Don’t worry! Check out these simple steps to make the transfer process easy and painless. But have no fear, transferring contacts from your iPhone 6s to an Android device is achievable even for tech novices. Here are some easy steps that can help you transfer your iPhone contacts to your Android device in no time at all.

Firstly, make sure that your Android device and your iPhone 6s are both charged and turned on. Then, turn on your wifi connection and make sure it is stable.

Next, you should sync your contact list to your iCloud account. If you already had your contacts in your iCloud, skip this step. Open “Settings” on your iPhone and go to “Cloud”. Then, log in or add your iCloud account to your phone. Now go to “Contacts” and turn on the toggle switch for “iCloud”. This will sync your contacts list on your iCloud account.

After that, sign in to your Google account on your Android device. Now open your “Contacts” app, go to “Settings” and tap “Import / Export Contacts”. Select the option to “Import from iCloud” and sign in to your iCloud account. Select the contacts that you wish to import and click “Import”. Your contacts should now be transferred from your iPhone 6s to your Android device.

Voila! With these easy steps, you can easily transfer your contacts from your iPhone 6s to an Android device. No need to be overwhelmed about the task anymore. So grab your phone and get transferring!

2. Pre-Transfer Preparations#

In this tutorial, we will show you how to transfer contacts from iPhone 6s to an Android. It is important that you have the necessary tools to ensure a successful transfer. To do this, you will need an iPhone 6S, an Android device, and a USB cable.

Once you have the necessary components, the next step is to connect your iPhone 6s to your Android device. Open the Settings app on your iPhone and select the “Bluetooth” option. You can then choose to pair your phone with your Android device. Once this is done, your devices will be connected.

The final step to transfer contacts from iPhone 6s to Android is to open the contacts app on your Android device. 3. Transferring Contacts#

In order to transfer your contacts from an iPhone 6s to an Android device, you’ll need: a USB cable, a computer with iTunes installed, and your Android device. You’ll also need to have your Apple ID and password, as well as your Android device’s Google account credentials. Once you have all these items in place, you can begin the transfer process.

First, open up iTunes on your computer and connect the iPhone to the computer using the USB cable. Once your device has been recognized, you’ll need to go to the Summary tab. Then, select Back Up Now to initiate a backup. After the backup has been completed, disconnect the iPhone from the computer.

Next, connect your Android device to the computer using the USB cable. Once your Android device has been connected, a window should appear asking if you want to transfer data over to your Android device. Select the contacts you would like to transfer over, then click OK to start the transfer.

2. Transferring Contacts with iCloud#

Transferring contacts from an iPhone 6s to an Android device can be done easily with iCloud. All contacts saved in the iPhone’s address book can be transferred without a lot of hassle. Here are the simple steps to transfer contacts from iPhone 6s to Android devices:

First, open the Settings app on your iPhone 6s and select your name. Then, click on iCloud and turn on the Contacts option. When prompted, enter your Apple ID and password and allow the synchronization process to begin.

Second, in the Settings app, navigate to Mail, Contacts, Calendars, and tap Add Account. Now, choose the Google option and enter your Google credentials. Again, allow the synchronization process to initiate.

Third, visit iCloud.com on your computer and sign in with the same Apple ID used to activate iCloud on your iPhone 6s. Click on Contacts and then select the Contacts icon located in the bottom left corner of the page. Finally, click on the gear icon and select Export vCard. Your contacts will then be exported to the computer.

Fourth, connect your Android device to the computer and copy the VCF file containing your contacts onto its internal storage. Lastly, open the Contacts app on your Android device and tap Import from storage. And that’s it. You have successfully transferred contacts from your iPhone 6s to your Android device.
