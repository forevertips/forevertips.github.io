2. Select Record from Settings#

Are you an Android Galaxy A13 user that wants to easily screen record and share with your friends? Look no further. Here’s how to do it:

First, open your Settings and select Record. Then, choose the frame rate you’d like - from 24 frames per second to up to 60 frames per second. Ensure you also check the ‘audio recording’ option if you’d like to record any sound emanating from your device.

Next, select the recording window size of your screen. Choose from full screen, adjust manually, or adjust to certain width and height dimensions.

Once ready, tap ‘start’ and you’ll see a 3-second countdown before the recording begins. You’ll also see a red indicator at the top of the display to signify recording in progress. To stop recording, just tap the same icon.

After you’ve finished your recording, head to your gallery app to review the file and share it with your friends. That’s it! 5 simple steps to easily screen record on your Android Galaxy A13.
