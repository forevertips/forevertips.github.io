1. Shortcut Keys yang Tersedia untuk Screenshot pada Windows#

Taking screenshots of your computer screen can come in handy to store important information or capture something you need to remember or show someone. Windows has several shortcuts to make the screenshot process easier. Here are the shortcut keys available for Windows users to take screenshots:

To save your entire screen as an image file, press the Windows key and PrtScn at the same time. Once you press these buttons, the screenshot is automatically saved in your Screenshots folder.

You can also take a screenshot of a single active window. To do this, press Alt + PrtScn. This will save the screenshot of the window to your clipboard. If you want to save the screenshot as an image, press Ctrl + Alt + PrtScn.

The third shortcut key allows you to select and take a screenshot of an area on the screen. To capture the selected area, press the Windows key + Shift + S. This will turn your cursor into a crosshair which will allow you to select an area to capture.

Finally, you can record a video of your screen activity. To do this, open the Game Bar by pressing the Windows Key + G. From here you can record your screen and save the video by pressing the Start Recording button.

These are the four different shortcut keys available for Windows users to capture their screen and save the image file or video. With these shortcuts, taking screenshots of your computer screen is much simpler.
